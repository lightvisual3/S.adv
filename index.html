About Me

A Journey Defined by
Perseverance

From a small-town dreamer to a dedicated legal professional — my path has been anything but ordinary.

"The law is not just a profession to me — it is a calling I answered with my whole heart, even if the road was long."

I am Shirin Sultana, born and raised in Patuakhali, Barisal. Life took me through many twists and turns before I finally found my true purpose in the legal profession. After completing my SSC in 2003 and HSC in 2005, I embraced the challenges life placed before me, never losing sight of my dream to one day stand in a courtroom and speak for those who cannot speak for themselves.

In 2018, I made the bold decision to return to academia — completing my Master's degree and enrolling in LLB the same year. That decision changed everything. Now, in 2026, I stand before you as a freshly called Advocate at the Narayanganj Bar Association, ready to serve with unwavering integrity.

I may be new to the Bar, but I bring with me the maturity of 41 years, the resilience of a life fully lived, and an unshakeable moral compass. ☕
